Biography

Jacob Hoffman is a D.C. resident who in 2016, competed at New Balance Nationals Indoor in the long sprints [200m, 400m]. In 2020, Jacob Hoffman graduated from Carnegie Mellon University where he put track and field aside to work on his specialty as a software engineer. Jacob moved to Washington D.C. where he works on AI/ML for the government, and trains in the long sprints at the Gallaudet University Track.
